.. _general_examples:

Examples
========

This gallery offers a curated collection of practical examples showcasing how
scikit-learn can be used to solve a wide range of real-world machine learning
problems.

The examples cover both general usage of the :ref:`API <api_ref>` and
application-focused tutorials that guide users step by step through common
workflows. For deeper insights, best practices, and extended explanations,
please explore the :ref:`User Guide <user_guide>`.
