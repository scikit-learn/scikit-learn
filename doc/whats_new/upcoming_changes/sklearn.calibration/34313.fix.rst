- Fixed :class:`calibration.CalibratedClassifierCV` with ``method="sigmoid"`` to
  convert :term:`predict_proba` outputs to Bernoulli logits before calibration
  instead of passing probabilities directly to the calibrator. For
  ``method="temperature"``, :term:`predict_proba` outputs are converted to
  multinomial logits. For ``method="isotonic"``, probabilities are kept on the
  probability scale. Estimators that only expose :term:`decision_function` are
  unaffected. Calibration now prefers :term:`predict_proba` over
  :term:`decision_function` when both are available.
  By :user:`Olivier Grisel <ogrisel>` and :user:`Antoine Baker <antoinebaker>`.
