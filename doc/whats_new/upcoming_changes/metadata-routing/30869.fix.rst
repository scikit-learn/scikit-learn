- Metadata is now correctly routed to the sub-estimator of meta-estimators such as
  :class:`feature_selection.SelectFromModel`, :class:`feature_selection.RFE`,
  :class:`feature_selection.RFECV`, :class:`feature_selection.SequentialFeatureSelector`,
  and :class:`impute.IterativeImputer` when they are used as an intermediate step of a
  :class:`pipeline.Pipeline`, i.e. when their composite ``fit_transform`` method is the
  target of the routing. Previously, metadata such as ``sample_weight`` was silently
  dropped in this case.
  By :user:`Adrin Jalali <adrinjalali>` and :user:`Kyle Schluns <kschluns>`.
